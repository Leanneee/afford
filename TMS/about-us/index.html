About Us

Tuition Management Systems is helping students and families afford education at colleges, universities and tuition-charging elementary and secondary schools nationally.

Easy to use affordability solutions are combined with your school's billing and payment process. Students and families simply select the options that best fit budgets and deliver the lowest cost. From pay-as-you-go plans to individualized counseling, these solutions work for everyone. Advice is provided whenever needed.

Serving millions of students and families over thirty years, Tuition Management Systems is committed to helping people afford education.
